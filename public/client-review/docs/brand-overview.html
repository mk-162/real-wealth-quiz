Real Wealth — Brand Overview

The core proposition

Real Wealth is a Manchester-based financial planning firm with a second office in Taunton, working with partners nationwide. The brand is built on a single idea, stated in their own words on the About page:

"We believe real wealth isn't just about money — it's about freedom. Freedom to choose how you live, work, and spend your time."

Everything else follows from that. The planning is in service of a life, not the other way round.

What the brand stands for

People, not products. The single most repeated positioning on the site is "Our advice is built around people, not products." This is the proposition, the differentiator, and the tone-setter all at once.

Confidence and informed decisions. The "What We Do" page opens with "we help individuals, families, and business owners make confident, informed decisions about their financial futures." The verbs matter — help, make, confident, informed. The firm isn't telling clients what to do; it's equipping them.

Insight, care and integrity. The firm promises to work "with insight, care and integrity — putting your goals at the heart of everything we do." These three words are the firm's character virtues and should be reachable in the tone of any app copy.

Long-term relationships. Real Wealth explicitly positions itself for clients "who value honest conversations and long-term relationships with trusted advisors." This is a firm that intends to still be working with a client ten years from now. App copy should never feel transactional.

Privately owned, long-term view. The About page references being "a privately owned firm with long-term vision." Freedom from external pressure is part of the story.

Community and civic role. The firm references "commitment to building a strong community of clients and local partners while playing a positive civic role in Manchester and Taunton." Place matters.

Who Real Wealth serves

Three broad audiences, surfaced consistently across the site:

  1. Individuals and families — including professionals and multi-generational families.
  2. Business owners — with both personal and business planning needs.
  3. Partner advisers — accountants, solicitors, and other professionals who want a trusted onward referral. The partner pitch is "One who values collaboration, clarity, and doing things the right way."

For the lead-magnet app, audiences one and two are the primary targets. Partner-facing copy belongs in a separate funnel.

Services in their own words

Listed on "What We Do":

  • Personal Protection
  • Business Protection
  • Pensions
  • Tax Planning
  • Inheritance Tax Planning
  • Decumulation Planning
  • Family Office Services
  • Cashflow Modelling
  • Investments and Savings
  • Succession Planning

The lead-magnet's "things to consider" list should use phrasing that maps cleanly onto this list so a user arriving for a discovery call sees continuity.

The primary call-to-action

Across the site, two CTAs recur:

  • "Let's talk about you." — used as the human-scale primary CTA.
  • "Get in Touch." — used as the functional button label.

For the lead-magnet app, prefer "Book a 20-minute call" or "Let's talk about you" over "Get Started" or "Claim Your Report." The register is invitational, not transactional.

Overall brand register

Quiet confidence. Warmth without sentimentality. No hype. No emoji. No exclamation marks in body copy. Serif-led typography communicating care and considered craft. Teal and orange as the emotional colour pair — teal for trust and depth, orange for warmth and activity. Photography of active, aspirational lifestyles — movement, freedom, the life the planning is for.

If a piece of copy or an interface element doesn't sit comfortably alongside the phrase "real wealth is freedom, and our advice is built around people, not products," it isn't on-brand.
