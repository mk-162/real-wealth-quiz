This notice explains what information we collect when you use the Wealth Conversation, why we collect it, who sees it, and how long we keep it. It is written in plain English. If anything is unclear, write to us at hello@realwealth.co.uk and we will explain it in person.

## What we collect

When you use this tool we collect:

- Your first name and email address.
- Your phone number, if you give it to us.
- Your answers to the Wealth Conversation questions.
- Session timestamps — when you started, when you finished.
- Consent flags — what you agreed to, and when.

We do not collect special-category data, payment details, or anything you have not chosen to give us.

## Why we collect it

We use this information for three reasons:

- To send you the shortlist of things worth a conversation, in the format you asked for.
- To prepare for a first call with one of our planners, if you book one.
- To improve the tool — so the next person who uses it gets a clearer picture.

We do not sell your information. We do not pass it to third parties for marketing.

## Who sees it

Only Real Wealth Partners Ltd staff see your answers. The team works from our offices in Manchester and Taunton.

The only third parties involved are the regulated infrastructure providers we use to run the firm — our email provider and our calendar provider. They process the data on our instructions, under written contracts that meet UK GDPR requirements.

## How long we keep it

If you do not book a call, we delete your session data after thirty days.

If you book a call and become a client, we keep your records for seven years after the relationship ends. The seven-year window is an FCA requirement for regulated client files.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

- See the information we hold about you.
- Ask us to correct anything that is wrong.
- Ask us to erase your data, where the law allows.
- Receive your data in a portable format.
- Object to how we use your data, or restrict that use.

To use any of these rights, write to hello@realwealth.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month.

If you are not happy with our response, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk or on 0303 123 1113.

## Cookies and tracking

We use first-party analytics only — a count of how many people start the conversation, how far they get, and which questions cause people to drop out. We do this so we can make the tool better.

We do not use third-party trackers. We do not run advertising cookies. We do not sell or share behavioural data.
