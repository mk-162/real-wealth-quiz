# S1 — Early Accumulator

## Banner headline
"The most powerful financial tool you have right now isn't money — it's time."

## Supporting copy
Every year of compounding at your age is worth more than a year of compounding at 50. The habit of saving matters more than the amount. Keep building it.

# S5 — Business Owner Growth

## Banner headline
"Your business is 45% of your net worth — and it isn't in this chart. That is the conversation this report is leading to."

## Supporting copy
The projection can only model what it can measure. The exit value of the business changes this picture by tens of percentage points — in either direction. Getting the plan documented is how you control which direction.

# S6 — Business Owner Exit

## Banner headline
"The numbers work. The question is whether the structure around the exit is ready to capture the full value — or hand some of it back."

## Supporting copy
On a £1m qualifying gain, the difference between the 14% and 18% BADR rate is £40,000 in tax — decided by the date on the contract. The structure conversation is the one worth having now.

# S7 — Pre-Retiree Affluent

## Banner headline
"The plan is done. What remains is a sequencing conversation — which pot, in what order, and what that means for the tax you pay across 30 years of retirement."

## Supporting copy
Retirement readiness is not in question. The value a planner adds from here is in the detail: drawdown order, state pension timing, the 2027 pension IHT change. Small decisions with long-running consequences.

# S8 — Retired

## Banner headline
"The runway is solid. The 4-year gap against a 95-year horizon is smaller than it sounds — and there are a few ways to close it."

## Supporting copy
The plan is in reasonable shape. The adjustments available — modest changes to drawdown rate, sequencing, and withdrawal mix — are smaller than most people expect. The legal gap (LPA) is more urgent than the financial one.

# S9 — HNW Multi-Generational

## Banner headline
"At 240% of target, sustainability is not the question. The question is what your family keeps — and the answer depends on decisions made in the next two to three years."

## Supporting copy
The financial plan is secure. The inheritance tax liability — approximately £1.9m at current estate values — is the one number on this page that structured planning can materially change. That is the conversation.
