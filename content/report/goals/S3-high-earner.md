# S3

## Goal 1 — Retire at 60 with no financial compromise

status: green
capacity: "Projected wealth 124% of the retirement target — on track with no change to current contributions. Time and compound returns do the rest."
rationale: "Well-positioned. The risk to this goal is not investment performance — it is the tax paid between now and then."

---

## Goal 2 — Reduce the tax on current income

status: red
capacity: "Income in the £100,000–£125,140 band is taxed at an effective rate of around 60%. A pension contribution or salary sacrifice removes income from the trap. No such arrangement is currently in place."
rationale: "This is the single most actionable gap on the page. It costs a defined amount every year it goes unaddressed."

---

## Goal 3 — Support adult children with property deposits or early inheritance

status: green
capacity: "Surplus income and investment position suggest good capacity to gift. The 7-year inheritance tax clock is not currently running — starting annual gifts now has no immediate cost and builds the 7-year record."
rationale: "Achievable, and the tax planning around it is straightforward to set up."

---

## Goal 4 — Leave a meaningful legacy to family

status: amber
capacity: "Estate is on a trajectory to cross £2m within 10–15 years as the mortgage clears and assets grow. Inheritance tax is emerging as a consideration — structured planning now preserves significantly more than the same planning in 10 years."
rationale: "The goal is achievable — but the amount the family keeps depends on decisions made before the estate crosses the threshold, not after."
