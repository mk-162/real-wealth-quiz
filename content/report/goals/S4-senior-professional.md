## Goal 2 — Travel extensively in the first decade (~£20,000 extra per year)

status: green
capacity: "Surplus above the baseline retirement target covers this with margin. The question is which pot funds it most efficiently from a tax perspective."

## Goal 3 — Leave £1 million to the children

status: amber
capacity: "Projected estate exceeds this threshold — but inheritance tax at the current level will take a significant portion before the family receives anything. Without planning, the net figure the children receive is materially lower than the gross estate suggests."
rationale: "The goal is achievable, but the inheritance tax planning needs to begin now to protect the full amount."

## Goal 4 — Be ready to support ageing parents if needed

status: green
capacity: "Income and liquid assets can absorb a meaningful level of parental support without affecting the retirement plan. The more important question is whether your parents have their own Lasting Powers of Attorney in place — not just yours."
rationale: "Financially supported. The legal preparation is the gap most families discover at the wrong moment."
